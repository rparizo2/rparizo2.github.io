SEO Type: Technical

Technical SEO refers to the optimization of a website's infrastructure to improve its visibility and ranking in search engine results. It involves various strategies and practices that ensure search engines can efficiently crawl, index, and understand your website.

Key aspects of technical SEO include:

Site Speed: Ensuring your website loads quickly is crucial, as slow-loading sites can negatively impact user experience and search rankings.

Mobile-Friendliness: With the increasing use of mobile devices, having a responsive design that works well on all screen sizes is essential.

Crawlability: Making sure search engines can access and navigate your site easily. This involves creating a clear site structure, using a sitemap, and avoiding broken links.

Indexability: Ensuring that search engines can index your pages correctly. This includes using proper meta tags, avoiding duplicate content, and implementing canonical tags.

HTTPS: Securing your site with HTTPS is important for both security and SEO, as search engines favor secure sites.

Structured Data: Implementing structured data (schema markup) helps search engines understand the content of your pages better, potentially leading to rich snippets in search results.

By focusing on these technical aspects, you can improve your website's performance, user experience, and search engine rankings.
