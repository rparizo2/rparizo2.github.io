Here are some highly recommended guides for learning SEO

These guides offer a solid foundation in SEO and are packed with practical advice to help you improve your website’s visibility in search engine results. Beginner’s Guide to SEO by Moz: This comprehensive guide covers all major aspects of SEO, from keyword research to link building and technical SEO. It’s a great starting point for beginners [5]. SEO Best Practices by Backlinko: Written by Brian Dean, this guide provides actionable tips and best practices to improve your website’s search engine ranking. It covers on-site optimization, keyword research, and backlink building [6]. SEO: The Complete Guide for Beginners by Ahrefs: This guide consists of seven chapters that cover the basics of SEO and provide links to useful resources from Ahrefs’ SEO Blog and YouTube channel [7]. Neil Patel’s Free SEO Resources: Provides a variety of guides, blogs, and strategy tips covering on-page, off-page, and technical SEO [8].
